Before you start writing, a few ground rules.
Aurthor is a tool designed to support and enhance your writing process. By continuing, you agree to the following terms:
Originality & Plagiarism

You agree not to use this tool to reproduce, plagiarize, or misappropriate any copyrighted or protected content. Aurthor is built to assist your voice, not steal someone else's.

No Harmful Content

You agree not to write, generate, or distribute content that promotes violence, hate, harassment, illegal activity, or harm toward any individual or group.

AI as a Tool

The AI in this app is your assistant. The creative work, voice, and responsibility for everything you publish belongs to you, the writer.

Your Data Stays Yours

API keys and all writing are stored only on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your content or credentials on any server.

Your Content, Your Rights

Everything you write in Aurthor belongs to you. We claim no ownership over anything created using this app.
